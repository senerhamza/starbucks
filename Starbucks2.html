History
- Our story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle, s historic Pike Place Market. It was here where Starbucks opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the classic tale, "Moby-Rick," evoking the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.

Starbucks: Iconic coffeehouse founded in 1971, offering ethically sourced coffee, a global presence, and a welcoming atmosphere for coffee enthusiasts
